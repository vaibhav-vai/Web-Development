Lionel Andres Messi

The GOAT

Here's a time line of Lionel Messi's life:

  • 1987: Lionel Messi is born on June 24 in Rosario, Argentina.
  • 1995: Messi begins playing soccer for a local team called Grandoli.
  • 1998: Messi joins the youth team of Newell's Old Boys.
  • 2000: Messi is diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency.
  • 2001: Messi signs with FC Barcelona's youth team, La Masia, after being scouted by the club's director of football, Carles Rexach.
  • 2003: Messi makes his debut for FC Barcelona's first team at age 16.
  • 2005: Messi becomes a regular starter for FC Barcelona and wins his first La Liga title.
  • 2006: Messi scores his first hat-trick for FC Barcelona and becomes the youngest player to score a goal in a Champions League match.
  • 2008: Messi wins his first Ballon d'Or award, given to the best player in the world.
  • 2009: Messi helps FC Barcelona win the treble (La Liga, Copa del Rey, and Champions League) and wins his second Ballon d'Or.
  • 2010: Messi wins his first FIFA World Player of the Year award and his third consecutive Ballon d'Or.
  • 2011: Messi breaks the record for most goals in a single season in Europe's top five leagues and wins his fourth consecutive Ballon d'Or.
  • 2012: Messi scores 91 goals in a calendar year, breaking the previous record held by Gerd Muller, and wins his fourth consecutive FIFA World Player of the Year award.
  • 2013: Messi wins his fifth Ballon d'Or and becomes the all-time top scorer for FC Barcelona.
  • 2014: Messi helps Argentina reach the World Cup final, but they lose to Germany.
  • 2015: Messi wins his fifth FIFA World Player of the Year award and his fifth Ballon d'Or.
  • 2016: Messi announces his retirement from international soccer after Argentina loses to Chile in the Copa America final, but he later reverses his decision.
  • 2018: Messi leads Argentina to the World Cup in Russia, but they are knocked out in the round of 16 by France.
  • 2020: Messi expresses his desire to leave FC Barcelona, but he ultimately stays after a dispute with the club's management.
  • 2021: Messi leads FC Barcelona to the Copa del Rey title and becomes the club's all-time appearance record holder. He then leaves the club and signs with Paris Saint-Germain.

For the world of soccer, Messi is a treasure because he is a role model for children around the world… Messi will be the player to win the most Ballons d’Or in history. He will win five, six, seven. He is incomparable. He’s in a different league.

-Johan Cruyff
